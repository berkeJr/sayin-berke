Love For Imperfect Things

Haemin Sunim

Listening openly, patiently, and attentively is one of the most significant expressions of love.


In the course of our lives, we sometimes become happy, and occasionally get sad. We also experience any kind of emotions such as anger, anxiety, excitement, enthusiasm and maybe disappiontment between this two opposite points of our emotional line. How we try to respond what we encounter in our lives usually reflects our personality. I intensely believe, after reading this book, it is really important to have the ability to just listen to in our relationships in our daily lives :) I also really love the half-poetic structure of the book, I think writer makes the book more fluent this way.

When someone you love is in pain, the most meaningful gift you can give is your kind presence. Sending flowers and texting are good, but not as good as sitting with her, holding her hand, looking into her eyes, and giving her a kiss.
